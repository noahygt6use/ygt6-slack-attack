Cultural values of Japan are very important to Japanese social interaction. The three most valued cultural values in Japan are harmony, order, and self-development. They are also a country with a vibrant "matsuri" culture, which is Japanese for festivals. Some popular foods in Japan include sushi, miso soup, udon, takoyaki, soba, and sukiyaki.


Paris has many foods that are very popular worldwide including: the baguette, the macaron, the crossiant, and escargots. Their culture is commonly associated with Paris, and it is the center of fashion, cuisine, art, and architecture. However, the life outside the City of Lights is different and varies from different regions.


Maine's culture is thousand of years old and millions of memories deep. It grew to include people and practices from Europe, Africa, and Asia. In Portland, they have many travelers from California and people who love indie and punk music. They are LGBTQ-friendly and love anything punk. Portland's most popular food are muffins, pastries, whoopie pie, and blueberry pie.
